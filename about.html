Need design work? A new logo? A whole marketing campaign? DIDA’s got you covered. DIDA is a student-run full-service marketing group, staffed by talented Dukies who can help you meet your marketing goals.

We are pleased to announce that DIDA is new and improved. There are 3 main changes that we have implemented.

1.

In-person consultations.
DIDA requires all clients to book an in-person appointment with our designers to discuss new projects.

2.

We have expanded our service offerings.
DIDA now offers wide-ranging services in areas such as branding, graphic design, marketing strategy, web design, and multimedia production. We have the capability of handling large multifaceted projects that span various media.

3.

DIDA now serves all recognized Duke organizations.
This includes undergraduate and graduate student groups, and Duke departments and offices.

Please note that DIDA cannot guarantee acceptance of all projects. New project requests will be evaluated each week by our project management team, and will be assigned based on availability. If the project management team determines DIDA cannot meet your requested deadline due to current work load and capacity limitations, we will contact you to discuss the deadline and the determine the status of your project.

DIDA services are free to the university community. The client will be responsible for all costs associated with production, printing, hosting, and other related project costs beyond payment to DIDA student workers.

If you would like to inquire about DIDA services please submit an official request. For other questions email us at dukeinnovativedesignagency@gmail.com
